About My Design Work

This space highlights my projects I have done by hand with no human or artificial interference. I've worked on these types of posters for a while, and I like making them minimalist and detailed at the same time.

Each design project starts with a random idea I want to work with, and then me playing around with the design and seeing what I like. This process also helps me learn what things I can do with the design. I use Canva Free to make these posters. Now, I know the possibility of the work I can create.
